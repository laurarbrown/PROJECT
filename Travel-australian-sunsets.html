“Travel to create a story worth telling”.
Traveling combines my two loves: exploring and stories.
This is what excites me most about photography. There is always something new to learn, somewhere new. I recently discovered long exposure photography and working on this in some dream locations, has been an incredible learning experience. I am driven to find out more and better my craft to create those stories worth telling.
